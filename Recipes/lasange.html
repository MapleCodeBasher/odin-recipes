lasange

This homemade lasanga is passed down from genartions and even good enough for my toothless granny and you can make it only a few simples steps steps and a few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Sauce
  • Garlic Granuels

Steps to make

  1. Mince meat and add seasoning
  2. put pasta sheets in a tray
  3. cook in oven 30 mins
