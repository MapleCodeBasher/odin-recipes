Saussage Roll

This homemade saussage roll is passed down from genartions and even good enough for my toothless granny and you can make it only a few simples steps steps and a few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • pastry Sheets
  • Sauce
  • Garlic Granuels

Steps to make

  1. Roll saussages into long lines add garlic
  2. Roll out pastry and put the mince in
  3. Roll the pastry back in
  4. cook in oven 30 mins
